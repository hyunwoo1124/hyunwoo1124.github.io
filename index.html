    • About Me.

    Hyun Woo is a skilled Security Engineer with 3 years of experience in IT & Cybersecurity, specializing in Azure Security, Vulnerability Management, Incident Response, and Detection and Response. He has successfully directed and designed a vulnerability management and remediation project, deploying scanners in remote offices and consolidating the data into a single dashboard for efficient action. As a member of the incident and detection team, he triages alerts and creates new detections based on Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures (TTPs). Additionally, he has architected Azure Security by implementing conditional access policies for all platforms, including Windows, MACs, iOS, and Android, to enforce MFA, password complexity, and software application packaging and automation using Intune Mobile Device Management. He has also centralized all security logs to Microsoft Sentinel as a SIEM (Security Information and Event Management) system.

    In his personal time, he enjoys writing pentest write-ups and conducting malware analysis. Looking ahead to 2023, Hyun Woo is focused on exploring data engineering in the context of security engineering, with the goal of optimizing search and detection times through the use of PySpark, data lakes, and data warehouses.
